Welcome to Barangay Calzada-Tipas, where harmony and progress converge.

VISION

"We envision the Barangay Calzada to be a progressive and peaceful barangay, wherein our constituents can live harmoniously."

MISSION

"To strengthen the implementation of good governance in our barangay and promote healthy living condition in our community."

Nestled within the vibrant heart of a bustling city, Barangay Calzada-Tipas is a charming residential community that embodies the true spirit of unity and progress. With its tree-lined streets, well-kept parks, and friendly atmosphere, this close-knit barangay provides a safe and nurturing environment for its residents. The community thrives on its strong sense of camaraderie, fostering a vibrant tapestry of cultures, traditions, and aspirations. From its dedicated local leaders to its engaged residents, Barangay Calzada-Tipas takes pride in its commitment to sustainable development, promoting education, healthcare, and social welfare programs. With its blend of modern conveniences and a warm neighborhood feel, this barangay is a haven where dreams are nurtured and lifelong connections are forged.
